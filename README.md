Shopspot 🛍
An AI-powered platform that helps small businesses create viral content and attract more customers.
What it does
Shopspot help small business owners -food sellers,thrifs shopps, and new businesses- generate ready-to-post social media captions,hashtags, and content ideas in seconds using AI.
The Problem
Most small business owners have great products but struggle to get visibility online.They can't afford a social media manager and don't know what to post.
The Solution 
Shopspot asks 5 simple questions about your business and instantly generate viral content tailored to your brand.
